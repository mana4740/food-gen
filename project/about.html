About Food Widget

Food Widget is an innovative platform that helps you find delicious recipes based on the ingredients you have at home. By using advanced AI technology, you can easily enter the ingredients you want to use, and Food Widget will generate a custom recipe for you to try.

How It Works

Simply enter the ingredients you have or wish to use into our search field, and our AI-powered tool, utilizing the OpenAI API, will create a unique recipe for you. The recipe includes the name of the dish, ingredients with measurements, and detailed cooking instructions.

Food Widget is perfect for food enthusiasts, home cooks, and anyone looking to explore new culinary adventures. Try it out and discover the endless possibilities in your kitchen!
